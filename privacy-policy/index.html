PRIVACY POLICY

We keep things simple. We do not track you, we do not profile you, and we do not store personal data about visitors of this website. This page explains what that means in practice and what happens only if you choose to contact us directly.

1. WHO WE ARE

UVATIME is a digital agency providing web development, Shopify, software, and mobile app services. Depending on the project, services may be provided by:

  • UVATIME S.R.L., Brașov, Romania; and/or
  • UVATIME LLC, Jackson, Wyoming, USA.

2. WHAT DATA WE COLLECT (SHORT ANSWER: ALMOST NONE)

When you simply browse this website, we do not collect or store personal data about you. We do not use:

  • Analytics tools (e.g. Google Analytics);
  • Marketing pixels (e.g. Facebook Pixel);
  • User accounts, login areas, or contact forms;
  • Newsletters or email subscription forms.

The only personal data we may see is the information you choose to share if you contact us directly by email or phone (for example: your name, email address, phone number, and details about your project). That information stays in our email/communication tools and is used only to talk to you about your request.

3. COOKIES & TRACKING

We do not intentionally use analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies on this website. The site is a simple presentation website, without custom tracking scripts.

Your browser or our hosting provider may use strictly technical data (for example, standard server logs for security and uptime), but we do not use these logs to identify you or create visitor profiles.

4. HOW WE USE INFORMATION YOU SEND US

If you contact us by email or phone, we will use the information you provide only to:

  • Reply to your questions or enquiries;
  • Discuss potential or ongoing projects;
  • Issue offers, quotes, or invoices where relevant.

We do not add your contact details to marketing lists and we do not sell or rent your data to anyone.

5. SHARING OF DATA

We do not sell or trade personal data. Information you send us may be visible to:

  • Our email provider or communication tools (for example, the company that hosts our mailbox);
  • Our accounting or legal advisers, where this is required for invoicing or legal reasons;
  • Authorities, only if required by law.

All such third parties are expected to respect confidentiality and data protection rules.

6. YOUR RIGHTS (ESPECIALLY IF YOU ARE IN THE EU / EEA)

Even though we keep data usage to a minimum, if you are in the EU / EEA and you have shared personal data with us (for example, by email), you may have the right to:

  • Ask what data we hold about you;
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate data;
  • Ask us to delete your data, where applicable;
  • Object to certain types of processing;
  • Request that we limit how we use your data.

To exercise these rights, just contact us using the details below and we’ll do our best to help, within the limits of applicable law.

7. SECURITY

We take reasonable steps to keep the tools we use (email, hosting, devices) secure and up to date. However, no system can be guaranteed 100% secure. We recommend that you avoid sending highly sensitive information by unencrypted email.

8. THIRD-PARTY WEBSITES

Our website may link to other websites (for example, clients’ stores or external tools). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those websites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies separately.

9. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

If we ever introduce analytics, tracking, forms, or any other functionality that involves collecting personal data, we will update this Privacy Policy and, where required by law, ask for your consent.
